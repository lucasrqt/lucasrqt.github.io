This page summarizes the courses I teach at the University of Rennes.

## 2025/2026

### Embedded System Security
- **Resp.**: Gwendal Patat
- **Lab sessions**: 30 hours
- **Level**: Cybersecurity Master (M1), *Cyberschool, ISTIC, Univ. Rennes*
- **Summary**: Introduction to embedded systems from a cybersecurity point of view including memory dumping, signal analysis with pirat buses and logic analyzers and reverse engineering.

### Software/Hardware Interface (Interface Logiciel/Matériel)
- **Resp.**: Laurent Perraudeau
- **Lab sessions**: 15 hours
- **Level**: 3rd Year of Computer Science Bachelor (L3), *ISTIC, Univ. Rennes*
- **Summary**: Introduction to assembly language (RISC-V) and CPU architecture.

### Unix upgrade (Mise à niveau Unix)
- **Resp.**: Angeliki Kritikakou
- **Tutorials**: 3 hours
- **Lab sessions**: 15 hours
- **Level**: 3rd Year of Computer Science Bachelor for Business (L3 MIAGE), *ISTIC, Univ. Rennes*
- **Summary**: Introduction to Unix operating systems with command manipulation and bash scripting.
