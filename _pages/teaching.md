This page summarizes the courses I teach at the University of Rennes..

## 2024/2025

### Embedded System Security
- **Resp**: Joseph Paturel
- **Lab sessions**: 18 hours
- **Level**: Cybersecurity Master (M1), *Cyberschool, ISTIC, Univ. Rennes*
- **Abstract**: Introduction to embedded systems from a cybersecurity point of view including memory dumping, signal analysis with pirat buses and logic analyzers and reverse engineering.

### Software/Hardware Interface (Interface Logiciel/Matériel)
- **Resp**: Laurent Perraudeau
- **Lab sessions**: 12 hours
- **Level**: 3rd Year of Computer Science Bachelor (L3), *ISTIC, Univ. Rennes*
- **Abstract**: Introduction to assembly language (RISC-V) and CPU architecture.
